# Objective 3: System-Level Intelligence and Demand Forecasting

**Owner (Member Name):** John Rey V. Bagunas  
**Objective Title:** System-Level Intelligence and Demand Forecasting  

## Objective Description
This objective introduces advanced data-driven capabilities, integrating the Google Gemini API for natural-language business insights alongside a Simple Moving Average (SMA) engine for seasonal demand forecasting and revenue monitoring. It is vital because it shifts the boutique's management approach from reactive "gut feeling" decisions to proactive, intelligent stock optimization and strategic planning. This module relies heavily on the structured historical data and booking information generated by Objective 1 and Objective 2 to accurately project trends, subsequently delivering intelligent reports back to the admin dashboard and powering the interactive AI Digital Stylist on the customer portal.
